About Me

My name is Forrest, and I'm not a big runner. I live in Austin, Texas, and am activitly growing my skills as a web developer. Thinking and building my ideas has been a passion of mine since I was a boy playing with Legos, and I have finally found an outlet where I can make my creativity a profession. The enjoyment from my work, along with the knowledge that I will always be growing and learning in this industry, gives me the fire to do my best each day.

Outside of being a developer, I love being outside and enjoying the beauty Texas has to offer. Most weekends I find myself hiking the greenbelt or at a lake or river around the city. Sports are also a huge passion of mine, my favorite teams being the Texas Longhorns and the San Antonio Spurs. I enjoy playing sports all sports as well, espically when I can combine them with my love for the outdoors.
